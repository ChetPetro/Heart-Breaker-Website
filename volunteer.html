Want to Volunteer?

We need many Volunteers to help us for event set-up, packet pick-up, event day activities, and event tear. We have opportunities available for each of the 4 days that are needed for the event each year, and appreciate any and all additional support that we can get!

You can download, complete and submit our Volunteer Application below, or if you have any questions send an email to heather.hall@hdgh.org
