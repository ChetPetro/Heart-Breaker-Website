Sponsor a Participant

Heart Breaker Challenge participants can raise additional money for the Cardiac, Stroke & Pulmonary Rehab programs by collecting pledges.

On-line option:
Simply share the personal fundraising link you receive from Race Roser for your “Donation Page” after you complete your event registration. You can also direct your family and friends to the Heart Breaker Challenge Race Roster registration page and have them click on "Pledge a Participant" to make a donation.

Manual option:
You can collect pledges manually using our event pledge sheet.

Remember to bring in your collected pledges and event pledge sheet when you pick up your Event Packet.

Corporate Sponsorship Opportunities

There are many ways to help support this local event and raise funds for these valuable hospital rehabilitation programs.

Corporate Sponsorship Opportunities

Event Sponsor - various levels of event sponsorship are available for your organization or business to consider. Sponsorship commitments offer a variety of event perks and marketing exposure.

Obstacle Sponsor - sponsor, create or build one of our event obstacles and have your organization or business name identified at the obstacle. This is an opportunity to get creative and have some fun before the event even starts!

In-kind donations - also very important in assisting with eliminating or reducing event expenses to maximize proceeds. Our event requires many different types of items that your organization or business might be able to help provide to us. You never know what you have on hand available to donate that we might need or could put to good use!

Contact Madeline Silveira at Hotel-Dieu Grace Healthcare for more information on how you might be able to help support the Heart Breaker Challenge.
